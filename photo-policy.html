Photo Policy

We love it when our customers take photos of their experience at Cafe Rina! However, we do have a few guidelines in place to ensure the safety and privacy of everyone involved.

  • No commercial photography without permission. If you're a professional photographer or taking photos for commercial purposes, please ask for our permission first.
  • No photography in sensitive areas. We ask that you please refrain from taking photos in our kitchen or other sensitive areas of the restaurant.
  • Respect the privacy of other customers. If you're taking photos of your friends or family, please make sure to get their permission first. And please don't take photos of other customers without their permission.
  • No photo manipulation. Please don't alter photos of our restaurant in any way, and don't use our photos without permission.
  • We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time, so please review it frequently. Changes and clarifications will take effect immediately upon their posting on the website.
