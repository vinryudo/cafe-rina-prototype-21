Reservation Policy

Reservation Required

To ensure the best dining experience, we require reservations for all parties.

Arrival Time

Please arrive promptly for your reservation. We hold tables for 15 minutes past your reservation time.

Cancellation Policy

Please notify us of any cancellations or changes at least 24 hours in advance to avoid a cancellation fee.

  • Call us at (555) 555-5555 to make a reservation
  • Reservations are available from 11:00am to 9:00pm, Monday through Saturday
  • We accept reservations for parties of up to 8 people
  • Please let us know if you have any dietary restrictions or special requests
  • We will hold your reservation for 15 minutes
  • If you are running late, please give us a call to let us know
  • We reserve the right to give away your table if you do not arrive within 15 minutes of your reservation time
  • For groups of 5 or more, a deposit of 50 pesos is required to hold your reservation
