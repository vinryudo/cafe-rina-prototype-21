Code of Conduct

At Cafe Rina, we value respect, integrity, and professionalism. We want to ensure that our guests have a pleasant dining experience, and we expect our employees and guests to adhere to the following rules:

  • Be respectful to others in all interactions on our website and social media pages.
  • Do not use hate speech, discriminatory language, or make personal attacks on others.
  • Refrain from posting or sharing any content that is illegal or inappropriate, including but not limited to offensive, violent, or sexually explicit material.
  • Respect the privacy and personal information of others. Do not share their personal information without their consent.
  • Do not engage in any form of fraud, spamming, or phishing.
  • Compliance with laws and regulations: We expect our guests and employees to comply with all applicable laws and regulations. Failure to do so may result in legal action.
  • Violation of any of these policies may result in immediate suspension or termination of your account on our website and social media pages.
